At pumpXcng, we understand that choosing the right fuel for your car can be confusing. We offer a variety of petrol (gasoline) and diesel options to meet the needs of your vehicle. Our most popular petrol choices include:

Regular Unleaded Petrol (87 Octane): This economical option is perfect for most everyday vehicles.

Mid-Grade Unleaded Petrol (89-90 Octane): This mid-range petrol provides a balance between price and performance for drivers who want a bit more knock resistance.

Premium Unleaded Petrol (91-94 Octane): Our premium petrol maximizes engine performance and is ideal for high-performance vehicles.

For diesel vehicles, we typically offer standard diesel fuel formulated for optimal engine performance. Additionally, we recommend checking your car's owner's manual to ensure you're selecting the right fuel type for your specific needs.
